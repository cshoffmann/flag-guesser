Flag Guesser

What is Flag Guesser?

Flag Guesser is a light, interactive game that tests your knowledge of world maps. Currently the game only features 27 curated maps from around the world. The flags chosen are from some of the most populous countries from the following five geographic regions: Americas, Asia, Europe, Africa, Oceania.

How to Play

Select your region and press the play button. Once you hit play, you will receive a word bank of the country names for the corresponding region, and a flag will appear. Select a country name, you only have one chance to get the right country however, so think wisely! You will be provided a score at the end to see how well you did.
