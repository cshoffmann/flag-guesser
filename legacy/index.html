Flag Guesser

What is Flag Guesser?

Flag Guesser is a light, interactive game that tests your knowledge of world maps. The game features world maps from 200+ countries and territories! The flags are divided into continental regions.

Flag guesser supports the following six continental regions: North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Oceania. In Flag guesser, carribean countries are considered apart of North America and the middle east region is considered apart of Asia. This is not due to any political reasons.

How to Play

Select your region and press the play button. Once the game begins, a word bank of all possible countries for that region will appear. Click on the country name that you think corresponds with the flag. You only have one chance to select the correct country, so choose wisely!
